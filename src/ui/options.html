Basic Usage:

  • On youtube, for every video uploaded under the "Music" category a "Show Lyrics" button will appear under the video (Looks like this: )
  • When clicking on this button a panel with the lyrics for the song will appear on the right (Looks like this: )
  • On other supported websites, a similar "Show Lyrics" button will appear in a prominent position on the page
  • When clicking on this button, the lyrics panel will appear on the page

Additional Options:

  • Use Ctrl + Shift + S on any supported website to toggle the panel
  • Click this icon: in the lyrics panel to translate the lyrics to any from the available 80+ languages
  • When the panel is open, click on the icon that looks like this to pop the panel out over the page
  • When the panel is popped out you can move it anywhere on the page by dragging it with your mouse
  • When the panel is popped out you can also resize it by pulling on any edge with your mouse
  • When the panel is popped out, click on the icon that looks like this to pop the panel back into its original place on the page
  • Auto-scroll
    • If the auto-scroll option is selected the extension will attempt to scroll the lyrics with the song
    • Auto-scroll can only approximate the relation between the lyrics and the music and could therefore scroll too fast or too slow
    • If auto-scroll is too fast or too slow for a song you can stop it for that songby manually scrolling or just clicking on the panel
  • Click on the panel, then use the right and left arrow keys on your keyboard to move to and heighlight the next or previous line of lyrics
  • New! Quadruple click on any line to select all the lyrics
  • Additionally, use the options in the "Options" to customize where and how the extension works

Autoscroll

  • When the autoscroll option is selected the lyrics will automatically scroll over the length of the song
  • To pause autoscroll, move your cursor over the lyrics. Autoscroll will resume when you move your cursor off the lyrics
  • To stop autoscroll, click anywhere on the lyrics. Autoscroll will resume when you change the song or refresh the page
  • If it scrolls too slow or too fast simply scroll the lyrics to the desired spot and it will begin scrolling from there
  • NOTE: when autoscroll is on, you cannot use the left/right arrow keys to highlight lines
